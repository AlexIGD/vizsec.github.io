Welcome to VizSec 2014

VizSec will be held in Paris, France on November 10, 2014. VizSec brings together researchers and practitioners in information visualization and security to address the specific needs of the cyber security community through new and insightful visualization techniques.

VizSec will again be held in conjunction with IEEE VIS.

The proceedings will be available in the ACM Digital Library.
